🚀 Launch Post April 9, 2026

Liora Media is live — here's why we built it

Today marks the official launch of Liora Media's landing page and the beginning of our public build journey. Here's the story behind why we started, what we've built so far, and where we're headed.

#launch #product #automation #socialmedia

Every product that exists today was born out of a frustration. Liora Media is no different.

We kept watching the same pattern repeat — creators and brand managers spending hours every week not on strategy, not on creative direction, but on the mechanical work of copying content between platforms, reformatting captions, finding the right hashtags, and praying they hit the right posting window for the algorithm.

"The best creators in the world shouldn't be wasting 40% of their time on content logistics. That time belongs to the creative work."

What we've built so far

Over the past months, the core team at AMNEXTECH has been designing and developing Liora Media from the ground up. Today, we're publicly launching our website and opening our doors for early access signups. Here's a snapshot of what the platform currently supports or is building towards:

  • ✅ Native integrations with Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube & Pinterest
  • ✅ AI-powered content generation for captions, hashtags, and post threads
  • ✅ Smart cross-platform scheduling with algorithm-aware timing recommendations
  • ✅ Unified analytics dashboard across all connected accounts
  • ⏳ Team collaboration & approval workflows (in development)
  • ⏳ White-label dashboard for agencies (Enterprise tier)

Who should sign up now

We're specifically looking for three types of early users in this phase: solo creators managing 2+ platforms, marketing teams at startups who need a scalable system without extra headcount, and agencies handling multiple client accounts who need white-labeling capabilities.

If you fall into any of those groups — reach out to us. Early users get priority access and direct input into our product roadmap.

What's next

We'll be publishing weekly updates here covering new features, integration milestones, and honest product lessons. This blog is a record of us building in public — the wins and the rough patches.

Follow along, hold us accountable, and if you want to be a part of this build — check our open contributor roles.
