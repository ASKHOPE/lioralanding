🌐 Our Story

We're building the automation layer for social media

Liora Media was born from a simple frustration: managing social media for a growing brand was a full-time job in itself. We set out to change that.

Our Mission

To give every creator, founder, and brand the unfair advantage of consistent, intelligent, and scalable social media presence — without spending their life posting. We believe your energy should go into creating great products, not into copy-pasting content across five platforms.

How it started

Liora Media began in early 2026 with a single question: "Why does managing social media still feel like a burden?" Despite dozens of scheduling tools existing, creators and teams still found themselves buried in the repetitive cycle of writing, formatting, timing, and re-posting content for every platform.

The vision was clear from day one — not just a scheduler, but a full automation engine. One that understands your brand voice, learns what your audience responds to, and handles the operational grind so your creative team can focus on strategy and storytelling.

Built under the engineering and product umbrella of AMNEXTECH, Liora Media is currently in its early product development phase — and we are moving fast. The platform supports native integrations with Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and Pinterest.

We are now opening the doors to our first users, early contributors, and investors who share our belief that the next generation of social media tooling should be intelligent, automated, and beautifully simple.
